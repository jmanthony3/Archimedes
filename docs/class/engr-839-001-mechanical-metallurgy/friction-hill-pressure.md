# Friction Hill Pressure

$$\begin{equation}
p = \sigma_{0}\exp(\frac{2\mu(a - r)}{h})
\end{equation}$$
$\mu$ is coefficient of friction, $r$ is distance from center, $a$ is radius of sample, and $h$ is height of sample.

# Interaction Energy

$$\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
U_{int} &= V\sigma_{ij}(\epsilon_{ij})_{T} \\
U_{misfit} &= \sigma_{p}\Delta V = \frac{1 + \nu}{1 - \nu}\frac{G\vec{b}}{3\pi}\frac{\sin(\theta)}{r}4\pi\epsilon r_{0}^{3} = A\frac{\sin(\theta)}{r} \\
A &= \frac{4}{3}\frac{1 + \nu}{1 - \nu}G\vec{b}\epsilon r_{0}^{3}
\end{split}
\end{equation}$$

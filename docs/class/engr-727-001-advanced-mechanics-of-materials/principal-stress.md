# Principal Stress

$$\begin{equation}
\begin{split}
\sigma_{1, 2} &= \frac{\sigma_{x} + \sigma_{y}}{2} \pm \sqrt{\bigg( \frac{\sigma_{x} - \sigma_{y}}{2} \bigg)^{2} + \tau_{xy}^{2}} \\
\tau_{xy} &= \sqrt{\bigg( \frac{\sigma_{x} - \sigma_{y}}{2} \bigg)^{2} + \tau_{xy}^{2}} \\
\sigma_{avg} &= \frac{\sigma_{x} + \sigma_{y}}{2}
\end{split}
\end{equation}$$
